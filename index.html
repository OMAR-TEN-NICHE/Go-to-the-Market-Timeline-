VIBE+ Marketing Strategy

Three-Phase Promotional Plan — Study Energy Patch @ VGU

Phase 1 · Awareness Phase 2 · Engagement Phase 3 · Conversion
1
Awareness
4 Months · Pre-launch
Attention ▼
Fanpage + TikTok
Social media launch
Build VIBE+ presence on Facebook Fanpage and TikTok before the product arrives on campus. Content focuses on student pain points — late-night studying, exam stress, lack of focus — using trending formats to build anticipation and early followers.
QR Code Campaign
"Energy Test" interactive quiz
Eye-catching posters placed around VGU campus with a QR code linking to an interactive energy quiz. Students discover their "energy type" and receive a personalised result along with a first-time buyer reward voucher — creating a direct bridge between awareness and trial intent.
Product Launch
Buy 1 Get 1
Timed to coincide with VGU's exam season, this launch-week bundle positions VIBE+ as the essential exam survival tool. Limited availability creates urgency and drives first purchases at the vending machine.
Goal Build brand recognition across VGU campus before the first sale is made
2
Engagement
3 Months · Post-launch
Interest + Desire ▼
TikTok Challenge
"8-Hour Focus Challenge"
Students record a 15–30 second before/after study video using VIBE+, then upload it to TikTok with hashtags #VIBEplus, #studytok, and #Finalsurvivor. The top 3 entries — judged on creativity and engagement — receive a Finals Survival Kit and a feature on the VIBE+ fanpage.
KOC Strategy
Education creator partnerships
Partner with KOCs (Key Opinion Consumers) in the study and education niche — online tutors, student influencers, and "study with me" creators. Authentic content around fatigue management and focus routines makes VIBE+ a natural part of a student's daily routine rather than a promoted product.
Goal Build social proof and create organic word-of-mouth within the VGU community
3
Conversion
5 Months · Ongoing
Action ▼
Flash Sale
Study Night Flash Sale (10PM–2AM)
A time-limited discount offered exclusively during late-night study hours — targeting students at their peak energy demand moment. Discount is capped at 10–15% to maintain positive unit economics above the 7,000 ₫ variable cost per patch.
Lucky Draw
"Mua là Trúng" — scratch & win
4-step mechanic: Scratch the card → Scan QR → Enter code "VIBE2026" → Spin to win. Prizes include free patches, bundle upgrades, and VGU-themed merch. Drives impulse purchases and repeat visits to the vending machine.
Loyalty Program
Focus Points system
Earn points with every interaction with VIBE+:
· Purchase 1 patch → 10 points
· Purchase 1 box → 150 points
· Share latest post on social media → 10 points
· Leave a product review → 20 points

Points are redeemable for percentage discount vouchers on future purchases, encouraging repeat buying and organic social sharing simultaneously.
Goal Drive repeat purchases and reach the 3,497-package break-even threshold within Year 1
